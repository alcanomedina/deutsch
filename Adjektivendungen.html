How to read this
Grey = free. Most endings are -en — Dativ & Genitiv are almost always -en regardless of article type.
Blue = easy weak. After der/die/das, the top rows relax to -e because the article already signals gender.
Colors = strong. -er -es -em appear when no article shows gender — the adjective takes over. They mirror der-die-das endings.
