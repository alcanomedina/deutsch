## Notes

- **hängen** already exists as transitive (Akk, hat gehängt, weak).
  This adds the intransitive form (Ohne, hat gehangen, strong).
  Both entries will appear in search — the translation
  "(transitive)" vs "(intransitive)" and different examples
  distinguish them.
