## Notes

- Strong/weak classification: bieten (s), frieren (s), treiben (s),
  beissen (s) are strong verbs; the rest are weak (w).
- bieten takes Dat+Akk (jemandem etwas bieten), though the Dativ recipient
  is often omitted.
- beauftragen commonly pairs with "mit + Dat" (jemanden mit etwas beauftragen),
  but the primary complement is Akk.
- klemmen can also be intransitive ("Die Tür klemmt") — the example sentence
  shows both uses.
