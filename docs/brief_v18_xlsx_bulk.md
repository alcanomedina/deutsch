reused an ad-hoc 8-category set. Neither held up under review
against the live `Substantive.html` (956 entries, 16 categories),
so before appending, the following corrections were made:

   Antwort, Duft, Erwachsene, Fuss, Grösse, Gruss, Massnahme,
   Massstab, Niveau, Sosse, Strasse, Typ (same word + gender,
   already present from V14–V17). 778 of the original 790 entries
   were appended.

   site's existing category, instead of adding a second, competing
   filter button. `Wissenschaft` went to `education` instead.
3. **Reclassified 88 entries out of `general`** into the site's
   existing finer-grained categories (body, data, education,
   emotion, family, law, society, time) where the word clearly
   fit an established pattern (e.g. Diebstahl/Mord/Gefängnis →
   law; Oma/Opa/Zwilling → family; Abitur/Mathe/Latein →
   education). Ambiguous words were left in `general` rather than
